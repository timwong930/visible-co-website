FAQ

Questions business owners actually ask.

No runaround, no jargon. Here's what owners ask us most.

Why is there a setup fee on top of the monthly price?+

The setup fee covers the heavy lifting: claiming and cleaning your Google Business and major directory profiles, verifying info, uploading initial photos, and fixing obvious website/mobile problems before monthly upkeep begins.

Can you improve our current site, or do we need a rebuild?+

Not always. We start by fixing what hurts customers most: outdated info, confusing service pages, slow mobile experience, and messy listings. If a rebuild makes more sense, we'll explain why and when.

How do you handle businesses with multiple locations or teams?+

Each location has its own profile, offers, and reputation signals, so we treat each as its own visibility profile with a smaller add-on fee per location.

What do I actually see each month?+

You get a short, owner-friendly summary: what changed, performance trends, reputation activity, and what improvements were completed that month.
